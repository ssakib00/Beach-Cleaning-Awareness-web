JUHU BEACH

Surrounded by the Arabic sea, and stretched to six kilometres to Versova beach. Juhu beach is the most affluent areas in Mumbai. It gives you the best glimpse of public on weekends or holidays as it is considered one of the best beaches to visit in Mumbai.

VERSOVA BEACH

Idle for swimming and jogging, the Versova beach is northwards from Juhu beach. Though, separated by a creek, Versova beach is one of the breeziest beaches in Mumbai, which makes it the best beach in Mumbai.
